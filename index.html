About me

Born in 1995 in Vrbas, but I grew up in Sombor, where I completed my primary and secondary education. I am currently 27 years old.

My passion for programming began in high school, where I specialized in computer engineering. That's when I discovered my love for coding and software development. In 2014, I participated in a competition for creating Android applications organized by Telenor in collaboration with Samsung. I am proud to say that I won an award in that competition, which further motivated me to continue developing my programming skills.

After high school, I enrolled at the Faculty of Technical Sciences in Novi Sad, studying Computer Science and Automation, to further deepen my knowledge in programming. During my studies, I had the opportunity to write articles for the magazine Play-zine.

In addition to my passion for programming, I have a great love for sports. I actively played basketball for many years, which not only provided me with physical activity but also taught me valuable lessons in teamwork, discipline, and perseverance.
